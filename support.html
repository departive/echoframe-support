Frequently Asked Questions

Why aren't my song suggestions playing?

Echo Frame requires an active Apple Music subscription to play songs. Make sure you've granted Apple Music access in Settings → Privacy & Security → Media & Apple Music.

What's the difference between Library Mode and Discovery Mode?

Library Mode suggests songs from artists in your Apple Music library, so recommendations feel familiar. Discovery Mode (Pro) explores beyond your library to find new music that matches your photo's mood.

Why can't I find certain songs?

Song availability depends on Apple Music's catalog in your region. Some suggestions may not be streamable in all countries. We prioritize widely available tracks, but regional licensing varies.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Echo Frame, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.

Are my photos stored or used for AI training?

No. Photos are analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded. They are never stored on servers and are not used to train any AI models. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why do I need to scan my music library?

Scanning your library helps Echo Frame understand your musical taste. This creates your "Sonic DNA" profile, which makes Library Mode suggestions more personal and relevant to you.

How do I delete all my data?

Open Echo Frame → Profile → Settings → Delete All My Data. This removes all saved Echoes and resets the app completely.

Tips for Better Results

Use photos with clear mood or atmosphere. Echo Frame analyzes colors, composition, and visual texture. Photos with distinct lighting, strong colors, or clear subjects tend to get more interesting matches.

Try Discovery Mode for variety. If Library Mode suggestions feel repetitive, switch to Discovery Mode to explore artists outside your usual listening habits.

Rescan your library after adding new music. If you've recently added new artists to Apple Music, go to Profile → Settings → Refresh Music Library to update your Sonic DNA.

System Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone (optimized for iPhone 12 and newer)
  • Apple Music subscription (for playback)
  • Internet connection (for photo analysis)
