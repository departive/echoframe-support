Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Echo Frame ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Our Promise: We collect only what's necessary to make Echo Frame work. Your photos are never stored on our servers, and your music library data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Photos You Analyze

When you use Echo Frame to analyze a photo:

  • The photo is temporarily sent to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis
  • The photo is processed in real-time to generate song suggestions
  • The photo is not stored on any server after analysis
  • The photo is not used to train AI models

Apple Music Library

If you connect Apple Music:

  • We access your library to understand your music preferences
  • This data is processed entirely on your device
  • Your library data is never sent to external servers
  • We use this to personalize song recommendations in Library Mode

Saved Echoes

Your saved Echoes (photo + song pairings) are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud if enabled. We do not have access to this data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose Storage
Photos AI analysis for song matching Not stored (real-time only)
Music Library Personalize recommendations On-device only
Saved Echoes Your personal collection On-device / iCloud
Preferences App settings On-device only

3. Third-Party Services

Echo Frame integrates with the following services:

Google Gemini AI

  • Used for photo analysis and song matching
  • Photos are sent via secure HTTPS connection
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Images are processed but not retained for training

Apple Music / MusicKit

  • Used for music playback and library access
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Apple App Store

  • Handles all payment processing for subscriptions
  • We do not collect or store payment information

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Photo data is transmitted securely and not persisted
  • Local data is protected by iOS device security
  • We do not store sensitive personal information on servers

5. Data Retention

  • Photos: Not retained after analysis (real-time processing only)
  • Music Library Data: Stored on-device until you delete the app
  • Saved Echoes: Stored until you delete them or the app
  • Preferences: Stored until you reset the app

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored within the app
  • Delete: Remove all your data using "Delete All My Data" in Settings
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable photo or music access in iOS Settings
  • Opt Out: Use the app without connecting Apple Music

7. Children's Privacy

Echo Frame is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you are accessing Echo Frame from outside Sweden, please be aware that your photo data may be processed in countries where Google operates its AI services. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
